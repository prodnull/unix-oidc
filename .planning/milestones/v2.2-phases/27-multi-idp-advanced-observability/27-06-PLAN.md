<objective>
Close the single gap found during Phase 27 verification: ISSUER_DEGRADED and ISSUER_RECOVERED events are emitted as raw `tracing::warn!/info!` calls in `IssuerHealthManager`, bypassing the `AuditEvent` pipeline. This means they receive no OCSF enrichment and are excluded from the HMAC tamper-evidence chain — inconsistent with all 14 other audit event types.

Purpose: Full OBS-06 and OBS-07 compliance requires these events to pass through `AuditEvent::log()` just like every other audit event. MIDP-10 health monitoring events must be SIEM-ingestible (OCSF fields) and tamper-evident (HMAC chain).

Output: Two new `AuditEvent` variants (`IssuerDegraded`, `IssuerRecovered`), constructor methods, `ocsf_fields()` arms, `syslog_severity()` arms, updated call sites in `policy/config.rs`, and a test covering all 16 variants.
</objective>
